Rabit Contract is the future contract-side layer for wallet-native authorization and more seamless execution patterns.

Today, its role is mostly directional:

| Contract direction | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| session-key style execution | can make execution more seamless than repeated manual signing |
| delegated authorization patterns | can support safer automation boundaries |
| stronger Solana-native signing flows | keeps the product aligned with wallet-native behavior |
